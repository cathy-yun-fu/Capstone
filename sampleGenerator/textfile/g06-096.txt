The reply was not bereft of asperity: 'Beaudesert,
Sept. 27, 1835 'Dear Melbourne, 'I have received
your letter of yesterday. 'I am not prepared to spend
the remainder of my life at Gibraltar, &amp; moreover
(if even residence were not the condition), having
no taste for a sinecure, I have only to thank
you for the offer &amp; to decline it.
