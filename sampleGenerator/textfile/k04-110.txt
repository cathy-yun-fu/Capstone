Except for their escort, Vittoria and
Lucia might have been the only living
creatures on an empty planet. After the
initial shock, Vittoria felt calm. She
knew exactly what she wanted, and
was prepared to take it without scruple,
if she could. What she had forgotten
in her self-absorption in her own schemes
was that other people were equally absorbed
in their schemes, which were likely to
run counter to her own.
