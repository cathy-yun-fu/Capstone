The price ranges for milk,
cheese, sugar, bread and flour
were very narrow. For most other
foods younger childless couples paid
the highest average prices and
large families the lowest, the price
gradients being steepest for
carcase meat (+7 to -7 per cent),
"other" fish (+8 to -11), "other" vegetables
(+9 to -9) and beverages other than
tea (+12 to -16).
