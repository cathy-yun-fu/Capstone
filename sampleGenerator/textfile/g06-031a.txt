'What a frightful event!' he wrote. 'I tremble!
What infatuation! Personally I am indifferent, but
I really tremble for my country! I may be
mistaken, 1tho' I cannot but fear that the
exasperation of the People will be so great at the
return of Ultratoryism, that the Commons House
upon a dissolution, which must be had, will be
a mass of Radicalism, &amp; then God knows
what may happen....
