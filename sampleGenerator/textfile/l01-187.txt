Nick and his grandfather shared their bedroom
amicably. The old man, though fighting a
grumbling rearguard action, permitted himself
gradually to become a little cleaner and
tidier. In his heart he was well satisfied to
have Nick's company. When they were alone
together he often explained all over again
how shrewd he had been in discovering
