'She is absolutely safe,' he replied; but he turned,
nevertheless, to the man who had peered into the
coach, whom he had addressed as Luigi, and gave him sharp
instructions on the matter. Vittoria stood looking about her,
breathing the scent of thyme. The land at her feet sloped
away into a tiny valley beyond which, on the crest
of a wooded hill-side, the ruins of a small temple
were etched against the clear blue of the sky.
