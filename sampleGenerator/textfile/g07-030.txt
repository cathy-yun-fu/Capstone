She had dreaded still more that he might
return to England, overshadowing her again
with spiritual and social peril. But this kind
of return was what she could never have
foreseen... that he # should come back not
voluble but silent, not beautiful but defaced,
not in obloquy but with his praises ringing!
She could remember now his exciting laughter,
his almost filial love for her, her almost
maternal love for him.
