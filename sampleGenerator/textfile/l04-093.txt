He lifted out the heavy bundles of notes and knelt on the floor
to count them. Of course, he remembered being in a great hurry to get
that fifty pounds for Kitty! Obviously he had been careless in
replacing the panel, but the possibility of anyone having
discovered his hiding-place gave him something of a shock.
As he replaced the bundles of notes, he withdrew the tin
box from its hiding-place and locked it inside the
cupboard.
