And I said to myself: Oh, God, doesn't the
boy ever relax! He turned his gaze away
from me almost instantly, but it had
been enough to make me conscious of the
nakedness of the upper half of my body, even
of the matted grey hairs on my chest. He
probably thought my appearance quite a breach
of the social graces; but, naturally, he said
not so much as a word about it.
