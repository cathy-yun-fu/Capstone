And we want all to eat and drink His Great Philosophy of the
four ways of Israel's redemption. Hence &quot;four questions&quot;, which are
really interrogations to examine into the principles of the four re-
demptions are asked at the Seder-evening about these &quot;four
symbols&quot; (the MANISHTANO). And the person who asks these
questions is seeking the opinion of the one who is
conducting the Seder.
