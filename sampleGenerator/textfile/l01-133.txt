"Mum, this is Maggie. I'm going to marry her,
and I'd like her to come and live with us."
Maggie was plump and plain with a pleasing
smile, a placid nature, and a slow-moving
but methodical mind. For twenty years she
had worked in the bottling-store at the bre-
wery, and Grace heard with astonishment
that Fred had known her on and off
for nearly as long.
