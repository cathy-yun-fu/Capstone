The moment had come. Upon the ensuing interview
the future would depend. Outwardly she was calm, but
her heart was beating fast, and the palms of her
hands were damp. Orsini's high-crowned hat with
its jaunty plume blotted out the light; his hand
was on the door. Glancing at Lucia he said in
French to Vittoria: 'Your maid ... is she reliable?'
