'In that case,' he reverted to French, 'I shall be obliged
to lift you from the coach.' Without answering him, she
rose, and bending over the agitated girl, said softly:
'No one will harm you, Lucia. Remain here.' Ignoring his
proffered hand, she stepped from the vehicle. 'Will you
be so good as to order your men not to molest my
maid,' she said coldly to Orsini.
