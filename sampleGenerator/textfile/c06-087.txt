Inevitably there comes the time when Mr. Hudson suddenly breaks
with tradition and arrives there in July, when, just as
inevitably, the place is full of American teenagers on an
escorted tour. Result: Mr. Hudson and lady love Lollo find
themselves playing chaperon (Brenda de Banzie, the official
one, has broken a leg) to the girls, who have just
been joined by a Jeep-load of boys.
