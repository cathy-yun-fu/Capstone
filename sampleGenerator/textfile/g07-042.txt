She had no desire to see in print that her
mother, who was to have been a duchess, had
been involved in a scandalous and ruinous
divorce, that her father, 'Mad Jack Byron', was
a profligate and a bankrupt who had
squandered every penny two successive wives
had brought him and left the second on
the verge of destitution, and that he had died
a drunkard and perhaps a suicide, hiding
in France to escape his creditors.
