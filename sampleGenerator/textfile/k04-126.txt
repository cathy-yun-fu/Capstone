She shrugged her shoulders, and replied in the same
language: 'Yes. But make it worth her while.'
'I see. I know how to deal with people like that,'
and turning to the cowering Lucia, he spoke in her
own language. 'Do you know who I am?' 'No,
signor,' she whispered. 'I am the Orsini. My word is
law in Rome.'
