The total number of dangerous occurrences reported
during the year was 1,409, an increase of 111 over
the total for 1959. However, the number of
notifiable accidents associated with occurrences fell
from 252 (31 of them fatal) in 1959 to
245 (22 fatal) in 1960. The main increase in
the numbers of occurrences reported occurred in the
category of those due to the collapse or
failure of a crane, derrick, winch or hoist, where
there was an increase of almost one-third from
335 to 438.
