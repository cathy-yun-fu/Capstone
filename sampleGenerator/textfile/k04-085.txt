This reverie was rudely ended as the coach gave a
sickening jolt and came to an abrupt stop, nearly throwing
the two girls from their seats. Lucia uttered a cry of
alarm. 'Look, madonna, we are surrounded!' It was true.
Men armed with pikes and daggers swarmed about
them and a lean, swarthy fellow was peering through
the window, grinning impudently. 'Santa Maria!'
shrieked Lucia. 'Banditti!'
