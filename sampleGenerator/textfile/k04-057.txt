Vittoria, accompanied by her maid, Lucia, was on her
way to the Accoramboni villa, ostensibly to pay
a daughterly call on her father, actually to coax
money from that indulgent parent to settle her mounting
debts. Lucia was thinking how beautiful her mistress
looked, and how cunningly the olive-green dress
with its underskirt of rose-brocade fitted her
perfect figure. Vittoria's thoughts were more complex.
